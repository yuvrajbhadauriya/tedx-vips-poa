Risk Management & Mitigation

Risk: Low Sponsorship Interest

Mitigation: Diversify lead list early (startups, local businesses). Create tiered, flexible packages. Leverage college network for introductions. Start outreach one week earlier than planned.

Risk: Key Vendor Cancellation

Mitigation: Maintain a 'backup vendor' list from the shortlisting phase. Ensure contracts have clear cancellation clauses. Pay advances only after signing contracts.

Risk: Technical Failure on Event Day

Mitigation: Conduct a full technical rehearsal on Sep 14. Have backup equipment for critical items (mics, cables). Ensure vendor's technician is on-site throughout the event.

Risk: Low Audience Turnout

Mitigation: Implement an aggressive, multi-platform marketing campaign with the Content team. Roll out early-bird ticket discounts. Plan engaging "teaser" activities on campus in the week prior.
